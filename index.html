Knowit Developer Summit

October 21.-22. 2016
Stockholm

By Developers
For Developers

Knowit Developer Summit (KDS) is Knowit's annual company-wide software developer conference. The 2016 conference will be held on the 21.-22. of October 2016 at the Radisson Blu Waterfront in Stockholm, Sweden.

Practical information

If you have questions during the conference, please feel free to contact us on Slack. We have created a slack-channel, #kds2016, for everything regarding the conference, join us and get the latest news! No account on slack? Then sign up first. You can also text/call +47 901 81 940 (Jan Henrik).

The venue. The conference area has its own entrance, to the left of the main hotel entrance. Look for signs!

Dinner. There will be a conference dinner at the hotel at 19:00. The dinner is included in the conference fee and is served in RBG Bar & Grill.

Name badges will be handed out in order to make it easier with names and be able to see which company you belong to.

Hotel check inn. You can pick up your room key in the conference area sometime after 16:00 on Friday.

Please stay until the end on Saturday. All speakers deserve an audience.

Be prepared to change rooms. We do not know how popular sessions will be. Perhaps we will have to switch the room just before a session starts. Please help us make it as smooth as possible.

Experience tells us that there may be changes in the schedule and maybe there will be a vacant slot. Do you have a talk that you can present? Please plan to quickly jump in as speaker.

There will be t-shirts for everyone. Please, just take one and take the size you specified in the registration.

Share! Use hashtag #kds2016 on twitter, blog on your company blogs and make this conference visible to everyone! There is lots of great content to be shared.
