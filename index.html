Knowit Developer Summit

By Developers, for Developers

26 - 27 January 2018
Oslo

Knowit Developer Summit (KDS) is Knowit's annual company-wide software developer conference. The next conference will be held at the Thon Hotel Opera in Oslo, Norway.

Register here!

Program

There will be long and short presentations and workshops in 4 - 5 parallel sessions and we will also include un-conference activities like open space which has been great success earlier years. Come up with something you would like to discuss and grab this unique chance to discuss various topic with your fellow peers.

We have not yet completed the program, but some of the suggestions we have so far includes Frontend development, Microservices, Machine Learning, JavaScript and a couple of workshops. Here you can find a list of all talks registered so far. We believe we have some exciting topics lined up for you. As before the motto of the conference is By Developers, For Developers!

Call for Presentations

We are still interested in more presentations, long and short. If you wish to propose a presentation you can still do so, but please hurry, we need it as soon as possible. The length of a presentation can be 15, 30 or 60 minutes. You decide! The subject for a talk can vary widely, here are just a few ideas:
  • JavaScript frameworks or frontend techniques in general
  • Mobile platforms like iOS or Android
  • Platforms like EPiServer and Office 365
  • BDD, TDD, DDD, Event Storming or other methodologies
  • Architecture, styles, patterns like Hexagonal, Event sourcing, CQRS, Actor model, Event streams or other patterns
  • Platforms like .NET or the JVM
  • Docker, Cloud, Orchestration (Kubernetes, …)
  • Security on all levels
  • Machine learning
  • Blockchain
  • Augmented reality
  • Tech culture and way of working
  • API’s in the real world. API management.
  • Performance or Amazing debugging techniques
  • GDPR - How does it affect you as an architect and developer?
... or any of a numerous of other interesting subjects.
If you want to register a presentation, fill out the form below and add the information about your proposed presentation. Right now, we are looking for speakers, and we will get back to everyone once it’s time to register.

The Conference

For the 5th time we are running Knowit Developer Summit, on 26th - 27th January 2018 at Thon Hotel Opera in Oslo. The conference begins with lunch on Friday 26th and ends on Saturday afternoon 27th. As before, the motto for the conference is “By Developers, For Developers!”
We believe this is a great opportunity to get better acquainted with others engaged in system development at Knowit. We think It’s important to participate in these kinds of events in order to create a good climate for cooperation across the company, in addition to the great program.
