Knowit Developer Summit

November 13.-14. 2015
Stockholm

Registration

Knowit's yearly company-wide software developer conference will be held on the 13th-14th of November 2015 in Stockholm, Sweden.

Register as a participant

Registration closes on September 7th, 2015.

The last day for submitting a talk was August 20th, 2015. However, if you have something really important to tell your collegues, feel free to contact us at kds@knowit.no. We might be able to squeeze you in!
